What is Manosthiti?

Manosthiti is a chatbot designed to assist individuals struggling with mental health challenges. It uses AI to provide empathetic responses and direct users to helpful resources.

Features

  • AI-driven conversations tailored to mental health.
  • Access to resources and support hotlines.
  • Confidential and anonymous communication.
